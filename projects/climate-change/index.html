Prototyping a story

This project is a prototype about your diet. How this has effect on the world and you. This project had to tell a story to teens around 13-16 that shows the problems with eating to much meat.

The hardest part was telling the story without having the solution that "eating vegetarian is the way". Storytelling was a important and creating a nice way to tell people they should eat less meat.
